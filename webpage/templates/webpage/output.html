Syndication

The Catalogue of Digital Editions was added to the German Datenbank-Infosystem (DBIS) for use in 317 libraries. DBIS was established at the University of Regensburg and is jointly funded by the Bavarian Ministry of Science, Research and the Arts and the German Research Foundation (DFG).

Awards

The Catalogue was second runner-up in the DH Awards 2016 Best DH Data Visualization category.
